# Analysis Phase 3 — Character (Skills 09–13)

> Phase 3 of 5 in the [Analysis Pipeline](../pipeline.md). You can run this phase independently if you have the prerequisites below, or after the [Narrative Arc](../02_narrative_arc/orchestrator.md) phase.

This phase examines who the characters are under pressure: how conflict operates at every level, what true character is revealed beneath characterization, the contradiction within each character, how the antagonist's forces push the thematic argument, and the deepest corruption of the story's central value.

## Stop protocols

### After Skill 11 — Pit stop: Character Dimension

*Character dimension* is the contradiction within a character — the gap between their public mask and their true nature. Confirm the dimensions before downstream dialogue analysis, which depends on knowing what's hidden beneath the surface of what characters say.
