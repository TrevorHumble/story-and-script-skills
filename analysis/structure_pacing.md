# Analysis Phase 3 — Structure & Pacing (Skills 14–17)

> Part of the [Story Pipeline](../README.md). You can run this phase independently if you have the prerequisites below, or after the [Character & Conflict](character.md) phase.

This phase examines how scenes are built: secondary narrative lines, the polarity turn within each scene, the rhythm of tension and release across the film, and where the gaps between expectation and result create surprise.

## Stop protocols

### After Skill 15 — Pit stop: Scene Values

*Scene value* is the polarity charge of a scene — the state that shifts from positive to negative (or vice versa) within a single scene. Confirm the scene-by-scene polarity map before rhythm and gap analysis, which both depend on it.

### After Skill 17 — Check-in

Lighter touch than a pit stop. Surface the biggest open problems from the structure and pacing analysis. The writer decides which to act on before moving to dialogue.
