# Analysis Phase 2 — Character & Conflict (Skills 06–13)

> Part of the [Story Pipeline](../README.md). You can run this phase independently if you have the prerequisites below, or after the [Foundation](foundation.md) phase.

This phase examines who the characters are under pressure: what disturbs the protagonist, how obstacles escalate, where the dilemma lands, how conflict operates at every level, what true character is revealed, and how the antagonist's forces push the thematic argument to its deepest point.

## Stop protocols

### After Skill 11 — Pit stop: Character Dimension

*Character dimension* is the contradiction within a character — the gap between their public mask and their true nature. Confirm the dimensions before downstream dialogue analysis, which depends on knowing what's hidden beneath the surface of what characters say.
